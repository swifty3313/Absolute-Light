About Absolute Light

Founded in 2020 by Terri Steele, Absolute Light bridges the gap between those seeking personal transformation and the talented healers who can guide them on their journey.

With many years in the holistic health field, Terri has built connections with a diversity of fascinating and effective practitioners to bring you exceptional experiences.

Our Mission

To inspire you to embrace your personal power, connect with loved ones, and enhance your health, wellbeing, and happiness through transformational events.
